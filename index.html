Tech Support for Nonprofits & Community Organizations

Technology should amplify your mission — not your expenses. We offer free and low-cost tech support to nonprofits based on organizational size and budget.

What We Offer

⚙️

IT Helpdesk & Tech Support

Reliable help for everyday tech issues — devices, software, security, and day-to-day IT problems.

⚡

Automation Solutions

Save hours each week by automating manual tasks like newsletters, volunteer tracking, and donor emails.

🌐

Website Design & Optimization

We build, fix, or enhance your site to look professional, load fast, and convert visitors into supporters.

📊

Data & Reporting Tools

Dashboards and simple reporting setups that help you demonstrate impact to funders and stakeholders.

🔒

Cybersecurity Basics

Help your team adopt safe digital practices with straightforward training and tool recommendations.

🤖

AI Tool Onboarding

Practical guidance on incorporating AI assistants and tools into your nonprofit's daily workflows.

How to Get Started

Reach out through our community channels or fill out the intake form below. A Tech Connect volunteer consultant will follow up within 3–5 business days.

📋 Services are prioritized for registered nonprofits and community-serving organizations in Alberta.

Our Mission

We're cultivating an ecosystem where innovation thrives through collaboration, mentorship, and shared growth — with equity and access at the center.

🤝 Community First

We believe in the power of mutual support. Every program, event, and service is designed to uplift the whole community — not just a select few.

🔗 Meaningful Connections

We facilitate genuine relationships between talent, companies, and entrepreneurs — not transactional networking, but real, lasting professional bonds.

🚪 Breaking Barriers

Whether you're a newcomer, a career changer, or a first-generation tech professional, Tech Connect removes the gatekeeping that holds capable people back.

🌱 Sustainable Impact

Backed by the AMG Foundation, we're building long-term infrastructure for Alberta's tech ecosystem — not a pop-up community, but a lasting institution.
